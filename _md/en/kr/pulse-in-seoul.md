!!! info "Organizer"

???+ venue "Venue"

???+ details "Details"

    Are You Ready to Feel the PULSE?  
    PULSE IN SEOUL 2026 is igniting the heart of the Korean Balboa scene!  
    The ultimate stage is set for Balboa dancers. This is your moment to experience and showcase the vibrant energy of K-Balboa to the world!  
    DATE: June 5 (Fri) – 7 (Sun), 2026  
    HIGHLIGHTS:  
    Live Music: Heart-pounding performances by our fantastic live band, Seo Hyo-Sang and his Hot Guys, to keep your feet moving all night.  
    Competition: Fierce and exciting competitions to crown the best dancers on the floor.  
    Instructors: Inspiring workshops with the world-class duo, Javi & Lucía.  
    Festival: An unforgettable 3-day journey united by pure dance and music.  
    This is more than just a party—it’s a true festival and a stage for your growth as a dancer. Don't hesitate to challenge yourself, and most importantly, enjoy the ride! Let your passion awaken the nights of Seoul.
